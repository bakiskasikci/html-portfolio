The Best Movies According to Bakış

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Green Mile

"The Green Mile" is an unforgettable film directed by Frank Darabont, based on Stephen King's novel of the same name. Tom Hanks delivers a captivating performance, portraying the character of Paul Edgecomb, while Michael Clarke Duncan's extraordinary power shines through his portrayal of John Coffey, deeply resonating with the audience. Fearlessly tackling themes such as justice, humanity, racism, and the death penalty, the film is renowned for its emotional intensity, atmospheric music, and stunning visuals. "The Green Mile" offers a profound cinematic experience, questioning the complexities of human nature and exploring universal values such as hope and forgiveness.

The Shawshank Redemption

"The Shawshank Redemption" is an unforgettable masterpiece. Directed by Frank Darabont, this film provokes deep thoughts on justice, hope, and human values. With outstanding performances by Tim Robbins and Morgan Freeman, it tells the story of a man unjustly sentenced to life in prison and his internal struggle, as well as the power of friendship. The film captivates viewers with its atmosphere, compelling screenplay, and performances, offering an unforgettable experience.

The Lord of the Rings Series

The Lord of the Rings is an epic story set in Tolkien's enchanting world of Middle-earth. The adventure begins with Frodo embarking on a journey to destroy the One Ring, and it is filled with rich mythology, unforgettable characters, and breathtaking scenes brought to life by Peter Jackson's magnificent adaptation.
